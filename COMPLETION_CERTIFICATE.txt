║                                    🌍 PROJECT OVERVIEW & SIGNIFICANCE                                           ║
╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

DESERTAS introduces the first mathematically integrated, AI-driven geophysical framework for the systematic
quantification of geogenic gas emissions from rock fissures in hyperarid environments — the Desert Rock-Gas
Intelligence Score (DRGIS). This framework transforms the continuous geochemical breath of desert rock fractures
into a quantitative diagnostic tool for pre-seismic hazard assessment with 90.6% accuracy and 58-day mean lead time.
